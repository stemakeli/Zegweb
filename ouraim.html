Our Aim at Zegweb.com


To encourage the use of code in static website development in a modern society where the use of "CMS" ( Content Management System ) is prevalent. We aim to keep website development simple, with easy to follow code but yet designing modern looking and stylish websites. We also strive to offer FREE static website hosting for your website courtesy of "Github" and "Netlify" so keeping costs down so any cost incured is purely for our services as web designers to put tobether the best website at a reasonable cost. And what's more amazing is it includes a "FREE" SSL certificate which most web host charge for.

Simplicity in itself


So you want a static website? Maybe for business, a hobby, just to let the world know that you have a service to offer. Do an internet search for website building and you will be bombarded with countless web hosts offerering their services. They will offer FREE websites but these are limited to "sub-domains". To take advantage of their benefits, you will have to sign up for a monthly fee, only then will you get a "FREE" SSL and your own domain, which you have a choice of buying from them or elswhere. There are plenty of sites available offering good prices for Domain Names. Whether going for a sub-domain or you own domain you will be able to use their CMS, the most popular beign "Wordpress". That's fine if that's what you want but you will still be limited in what you can design unless you go for a premium package to open up all features.
Our site aims to give you a full featured static site for a fraction of the cost compared to signing up for a web host - CMS - SSL - yearly fees - leading to more complex code ( in the background ) with more complex breakages, meaning having to spend more to put it right. Keeping it simple is simplicity in itself!
